What is LiftSync?

Built by a lifter, for lifters.

LiftSync is a calendar-based workout tracking app designed to make progressive overload effortless. Think of it like MyFitnessPal — but built entirely around your training, not your food.

Every session you log becomes data. Every rep, every set, every weight increase is tracked so you always know exactly where you left off and what to push for next time.

No more guessing. No more forgotten PRs. Just consistent, measurable progress.
